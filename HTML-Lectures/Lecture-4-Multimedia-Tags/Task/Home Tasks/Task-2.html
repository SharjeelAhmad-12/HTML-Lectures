Introduction

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

Follow these steps:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy. Add crushed garlic for flavor.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta and toss it with the pancetta and garlic in the pan.
  5. Turn off the heat, add the egg mixture, and stir quickly to coat the pasta without scrambling the eggs.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve immediately!
