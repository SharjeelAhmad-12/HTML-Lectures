Chocolate Cake Recipe

This chocolate cake recipe is perfect for all dessert lovers!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps to Cook

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, butter, and vanilla extract to the dry mix and stir well.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving.

Cooking Time: 45 minutes
