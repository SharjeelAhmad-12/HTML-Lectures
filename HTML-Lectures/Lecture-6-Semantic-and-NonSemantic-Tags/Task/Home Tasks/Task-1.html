Chapter 1: Forces

In this chapter, we will explore the concept of force, how it affects the motion of objects, and the different types of forces that exist.

Key Concepts:

  • Force is a push or pull on an object.
  • There are different types of forces: contact forces and non-contact forces.
  • Newton's 3 laws of motion describe the relationship between force and motion.

Key Terms:

Force
A push or pull on an object that can cause it to move or change its velocity.
Contact Force
A force that requires two objects to be in physical contact.

Chapter 2: Motion

This chapter focuses on the various aspects of motion, including velocity, acceleration, and the relationships between these quantities.

Key Concepts:

  • Motion refers to the change in position of an object over time.
  • Velocity is the speed of an object in a given direction.
  • Acceleration occurs when there is a change in velocity over time.

Chapter 3: Energy

In this chapter, we will explore the various forms of energy and how they are transferred between systems.

Key Concepts:

  • Energy is the capacity to do work.
  • There are various forms of energy: kinetic, potential, thermal, and chemical.
  • The law of conservation of energy states that energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed.
