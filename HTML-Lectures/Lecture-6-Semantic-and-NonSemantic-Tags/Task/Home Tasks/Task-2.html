Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian pasta dish with a rich, savory sauce made from ground beef, tomatoes, and spices.

Ingredients

  1. 200g ground beef
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 400g canned tomatoes
  5. 200g spaghetti
  6. Olive oil
  7. Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© onions and garlic until softened.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in canned tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and garnish with basil leaves.
